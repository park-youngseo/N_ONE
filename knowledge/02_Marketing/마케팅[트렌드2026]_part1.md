Introduction
The Great Content Shift
The B2B content landscape is experiencing a seismic shift that most brands aren’t prepared
for. NerdWallet’s 18% traffic drop is just the beginning of a larger trend where AI will consume
traditional informational content. Companies that adapt quickly to personality-driven content
will capture disproportionate market share, while those clinging to old methods will see
diminishing returns.

Talent System
Finding and developing content creators is fundamentally different from hiring
traditional marketing roles. Instead of looking for established influencers, focus on
subject matter experts who show potential for personality-driven content. The best
creators often come from technical or specialist roles where they’ve developed
deep expertise but haven’t yet found their voice.
Development Framework:
1 Talent Identification 2 Creator Development
• Subject expertise first • Platform-specific training
• Communication potential • Storytelling workshops
• Learning capacity • Brand voice alignment
• Cultural alignment • Performance coaching

Success Metrics
Traditional content metrics like pageviews and time on page won’t capture the full
impact of personality-driven content. Your measurement framework needs to evolve
to track both creator success and brand impact. Focus on metrics that measure
audience loyalty and engagement depth rather than just surface-level reach.
Measurement Framework:
1 Creator Impact 2 Brand Health 3 Business Results
• Audience growth • Message resonance • Lead quality
• Engagement rates • Share of voice • Sales velocity
• Brand sentiment • Revenue impact
• Community building
• Audience loyalty • Customer retention
• Content consistency
