Building Your Personal Brand
Consider the concept of personal selling, and apply it to yourself. What information would you need? How
would you go about crafting your message? First, gather the facts that you would like someone to know about
you, and create a list. Next, craft the basic “elevator pitch” introduction (that is, you only have an elevator ride

Next, make a list of the situations where you might need an introduction (sales pitch) that is longer and
contains more detail. Consider a job interview (much of this information should be on your resume), joining a
