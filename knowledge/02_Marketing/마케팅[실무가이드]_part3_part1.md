Page features
The Page features make it simple for public figures, businesses and creators to build
community and achieve their objectives. With a Page, you can create posts and ads along with

Simple layout

● Having separate personal and business presences enables you to switch into your
business presence and engage on a number of surfaces as your business.

You can grant access to people who manage your Page and control what level of access they

content, messages, community activity, ads and insights.
Business insights
You can access insights that can help you understand how your business presence is doing,
